Hercule Poirot, the renowned Belgian detective known for
his impressive skills of observation and intellect, receives a
rather curious invitation out of the blue. It comes from Mr.
Shaitana, a man of exotic tastes and mysterious aura, who is
infamous in London's high society. Mr. Shaitana is known
for collecting unusual items and, seemingly, unusual people.
